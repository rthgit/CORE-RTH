# Contributing to Core Rth

Thank you for your interest in contributing to the Sovereign Cognitive Kernel!

## Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO)

By contributing to this project (submitting a pull request, patch, or code snippet), you certify that:
1. You created the contribution or have the right to submit it.
2. You agree that your contribution is licensed under the same terms as the **Core Rth Source-Available License**.
3. You grant the Author a perpetual, irrevocable, worldwide license to use, modify, and distribute your contributions under the terms of the project's license.

To sign off on your contribution, please include the following line at the end of your commit message:

`Signed-off-by: Your Name <your.email@example.com>`
